## 1️⃣ Introduction

### What is Memora AI?

**Memora AI** is an AI-powered, secure digital document management platform designed to become a person's **permanent digital memory**.

Unlike traditional cloud storage platforms that simply store files, Memora AI **understands** every uploaded document, extracts important information, organizes it intelligently, answers questions about it, reminds users of upcoming deadlines, and assists with real-world tasks such as:

- 🎓 Job applications
- 🛂 Passport renewals
- 📚 Scholarship applications
- 🏥 Insurance claims
- and much more...

Think of it as a combination of:

```
Google Drive  +  DigiLocker  +  ChatGPT  +  Dropbox  +  Notion
```

**combined into one intelligent assistant.**

Instead of remembering *where* documents are stored, users simply **ask Memora AI**.

### Example Queries

```
"Show my PAN card."
"When does my passport expire?"
"Generate my resume."
"Find every document containing my old address."
```

## 4️⃣ Problem Statement

Today, every person stores documents across **multiple locations**:

```
📁 Google Drive    📁 Downloads Folder    📧 Email Attachments
💬 WhatsApp        📨 Telegram            🖥️ Desktop
💾 Pendrive        💿 Hard Disk           🏛️ DigiLocker
```

This creates **five major problems**:

---

### ❌ Problem 1 — Finding Documents Takes Too Much Time

> People often spend **15–30 minutes** searching for a single document.

**Example:** A student needs to upload their degree certificate.

```
Search Downloads
      ↓
Search Desktop
      ↓
Search Google Drive
      ↓
Search WhatsApp
      ↓
Search Email
      ↓
Finally finds it. ← Time wasted.
```

---

### ❌ Problem 2 — Important Documents Expire

Users forget expiry dates for:

| Document | Consequence of Missing |
|----------|----------------------|
| 🛂 Passport | Travel disrupted |
| 🚗 Driving Licence | Legal penalties |
| 🏥 Insurance | Coverage lapse |
| 💊 Medical Prescription | Health risk |
| 🎓 College Fee | Enrollment cancelled |
| 🧾 Income Tax | Penalty & interest |
| 🏦 EMI | Credit score damaged |

> Users usually remember **only after** the expiry date.

---

### ❌ Problem 3 — Repeating Information

Every application form asks for the same data:

```
Name | Address | DOB | PAN | Passport | Phone | Email
```

> Users manually type the **same information hundreds of times**.

---

### ❌ Problem 4 — Unsafe Document Sharing

People share sensitive documents through:

- 💬 WhatsApp
- 📧 Email
- 📨 Telegram
- 🔗 Permanent Google Drive links

**Why this is dangerous:**
- Documents remain permanently accessible
- Personal information may be leaked
- Anyone with the link can download the document

---

### ❌ Problem 5 — Traditional Cloud Storage is Passive

| Platform | What it Does | What it Lacks |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Google Drive | Stores files | No AI understanding |
| Dropbox | Stores files | No reminders |
| OneDrive | Stores files | Manual search only |

> None of them truly **understand** what is inside those files. Searching still depends on filenames or folders.
